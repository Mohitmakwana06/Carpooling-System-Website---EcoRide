Who Are We?

Welcome to Treats With You, a dessert shop that specializes in high-quality homemade desserts. They offer classic treats like chocolate chip cookies and brownies, as well as unique options like macarons and cupcakes. Their signature cake, the "Treats With You Special," is popular, and they also have fresh fruit tarts and pies for those seeking lighter options. They provide gluten-free and vegan choices to ensure that everyone can enjoy a sweet treat. So come visit us at Treats With You and let us make your day a little sweeter.
